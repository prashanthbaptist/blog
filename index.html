Ichigo Ichie! - This moment

"Ichigo Ichie" is the Japanese idea that, what we experience in the "now" will never repeat itself in the same way ever again. We human beings spend a lot of time dwelling either in the past or in the future. Emotions drift us from the present. Hector and Francesc explore in this book how to be in the present, stay calm, exercise control and remain focussed in this information age.
